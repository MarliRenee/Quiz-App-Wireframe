Intro to Accessibility

Content is the most important part of your site.

When writing content, you should:

  • Use plain language and avoid figures of speech, idioms, and complicated metaphors.
  • Pretend it’s 1860 and you’re being paid by the word! See how many lengthy descriptive adjectives you can add.
  • Assume most of your readers are middle-age astrophysicists.
  • Are words even necessary?
